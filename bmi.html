What is BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple calculation using height and weight to find out if a person is underweight, healthy, overweight, or obese.

BMI Weight Status
Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5 – 24.9 Healthy
25.0 – 29.9 Overweight
30.0 and Above Obese
